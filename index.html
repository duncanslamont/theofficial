Many of my novel and screenplay ideas have shattered before my eyes as I read these books, but a few stand strong. So this voice would not transfer when audio is not present, but obviously people read books in their heads so it’s obviously not ruined, just not maximized(though depending on the actors, it might not be maximized in film either).
Anyways, I have thoughts of perhaps trying to do stand up when I get back to NYC, though I don’t really have an in, and also there is a virus raging on. I could ask my kind-of friend who is apparently a stand up, though I never thought she was that funny. Time will tell, but these voices I have spent time developing need to be shared with the world, and I think they can occasionally be so good they warrant a laugh without good content. And good content isn’t all that hard to come by. But we’ll see. Not a good time to start being a stand up.
Anyways these are just some of my thoughts, a new kimono for a while. Been writing a journal, as well as a dream journal. It’s fun. No way I remember my dream last night if I didn’t write it down. Alright, adios. Email me at thewalrusauce@gmail.com if you wish!
